Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna will make your mouth watering. Garfield would not resist.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground meat
  • Italian spices
  • Lasagnas
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Put sauce at the bottom of a glass container
  2. Put a first layer of lasagnas
  3. Add sauce on top of the lasagna
  4. Repeat process two times
  5. Add cheese on top
